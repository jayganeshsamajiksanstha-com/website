About Our Organization

Registered NGO Working for Social Change

Jay Ganesh Samajik Bhoudeshiy Sanstha is a registered non-profit organization established in 2006 under the Societies Registration Act, 1860. Our organization is committed to the holistic development of rural communities in Maharashtra.

We are registered under Section 12A and 80G of the Income Tax Act, making all donations eligible for tax exemption. Our NITI Aayog UID is MH/2017/0152698.

Our mission is to empower underprivileged communities through education, skill development, and sustainable livelihood programs. We work across multiple districts including Solapur, Osmanabad, Beed, Sangli, Pune, Satara, and Ahmednagar.

With a dedicated team of trustees and volunteers, we have successfully implemented various projects focused on education, women's skill development, farmer welfare, and community banking.

Refund Policy

Jay Ganesh Samajik Bhoudeshiy Sanstha has the following refund policy for donations:

  1. Donations are generally non-refundable as they support charitable activities.
  2. Refunds may be considered only in cases of technical errors resulting in duplicate transactions or incorrect amounts.
  3. Refund requests must be submitted in writing within 7 days of the transaction date.
  4. Approved refunds will be processed within 15 working days through the original payment method.
  5. Once a tax exemption certificate (80G) has been issued for a donation, it cannot be refunded.
  6. The organization reserves the right to make final decisions on all refund requests.

80G Tax Exemption

Jay Ganesh Samajik Bhoudeshiy Sanstha is registered under Section 12A and 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

  1. All donations made to our organization are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G.
  2. Donors will receive an official 80G receipt via email within 15 days of their donation.
  3. The 80G receipt can be used to claim tax deduction while filing income tax returns.
  4. There is no upper limit on the amount that can be claimed as deduction under Section 80G.
  5. Our 80G registration number is: PN/CTT(EXEMP)/TECH/80G/771/2017-18/614
  6. For any queries regarding 80G receipts, please contact us at hindavieducation@gmail.com
